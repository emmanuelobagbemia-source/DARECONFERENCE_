Our Story

A vision to help teenagers discover the rare energy already inside them.

DARE Conference was conceived by Mummy Arin with a simple but powerful vision: to help teenagers discover who they truly are, recognize the unique gifts God has placed inside them, and become people who make meaningful impact in their generation. The name DARE reflects that vision, encouraging young people to discover the uncommon abilities, purpose and potential that God has deposited within them.

For eleven consecutive years, DARE Conference has remained committed to raising teenagers who are confident in their identity, intentional about their growth and equipped to do great exploits through Christ. Every edition builds on the one before it, calling a new wave of young people to step into who they were created to be.

The Meaning

What Does DARE Mean?

Discovering All Rare Energy represents the vision of helping teenagers discover the God given gifts, purpose, creativity, leadership and potential placed within them. It is a call to awaken every young person to the unique rare energy God has deposited in their lives so they can grow, serve, lead and make lasting impact.
